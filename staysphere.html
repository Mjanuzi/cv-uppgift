Staysphere

Discription

Staysphere is an application where individuals can list their private accommodations for rent, alongside hotel listings. The goal is to give users the flexibility to choose between booking a hotel room or a privately owned home.

Users can create an account, log in, and use the platform to browse and book available stays. In addition to booking, users can also list their own property for others to rent.

As a private host, you have full control over your listing – you choose the available dates and manually approve or reject booking requests through simple “Accept” or “Reject” buttons. This adds an extra layer of security and personalization.

For hotels, however, bookings are processed automatically without the need for approval, streamlining the process for larger businesses.

Project type

Fullstack project

Crew

For this project, we were a team of three students working together through all parts of the development process — from database design and backend development to the frontend interface.

We followed agile methodologies, with daily stand-up meetings and weekly sprints to plan, prioritize, and reflect on our progress. This helped us stay organized, adapt to challenges quickly, and ensure continuous delivery throughout the project.
